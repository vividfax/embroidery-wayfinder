Embroidery Wayfinder

A tool that finds the closest matching embroidery floss for a specific colour from a colour picker.

Includes floss from:
DMC, Cosmo, Anchor

Based on the yarn Wayfinder. Works best on desktop. Huge thanks to NaejDoree, Greg Randall and Lord Libidan for their help adding flosses!

Spotted a typo or found that a floss colour is inconsistent? Feel free to message me @vividfax on twitter and instagram.
